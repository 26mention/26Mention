How to Order:

Welcome to Rahul Book Point. Follow these steps to place your order:

  1. Search or Find Product: Browse through categories to find the product you want.
  2. Add to Cart: Click on the product and select the 'Add to Cart' button.
  3. Proceed to Checkout: Once you have added products, click the 'Next' button to proceed.
  4. Choose Payment Method: Select your preferred payment method:
    • Cash on Delivery (COD)
    • Online Payment
  5. If Online: Attach a screenshot of your payment.
  6. Place Order: Click the 'Place Order' button to complete your purchase.

NOTE: After placing your order, you will receive an email with all product details and the expected time for pickup at our store.
